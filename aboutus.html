Advocate Ashish Raj

Advocate Ashish Raj is a practicing lawyer enrolled with the Bar Council of Delhi, appearing regularly before the Hon’ble High Court of Delhi, District Courts, and various tribunals across India. His practice encompasses a wide spectrum of legal matters, with particular focus on civil litigation, writ jurisdiction, constitutional issues, and criminal law. He has also developed expertise in drafting, advisory, and dispute resolution, offering clients comprehensive legal support tailored to their individual requirements.
