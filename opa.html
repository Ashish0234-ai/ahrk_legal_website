Our Practice Area

Criminal Litigation

We provide representation in criminal matters across all judicial forums, including the Supreme Court, High Courts, and trial courts. Our practice includes bail applications, anticipatory bail, FIR matters, trial proceedings, appeals, revisions, and matters under special legislations such as the Prevention of Corruption Act, POCSO Act, and NDPS Act. We focus on accurate interpretation of statutes, procedural compliance, and careful preparation of legal arguments. Our work encompasses both defense and representation of victims, ensuring that all actions are conducted in accordance with law. We handle documentation, evidence management, and coordination with courts, prosecutors, and relevant authorities while adhering to legal and ethical standards. Our practice is structured to provide clarity and guidance to clients at each stage of the criminal process. By combining knowledge of statutory provisions, case law, and procedural requirements, we provide comprehensive representation while maintaining confidentiality and professional ethics. Writs & Constitutional Matters We have experience in filing and arguing writ petitions and constitutional remedies before the Supreme Court, High Courts, and tribunals. Our practice covers habeas corpus, mandamus, certiorari, prohibition, and quo warranto matters, addressing issues related to fundamental rights, administrative actions, statutory compliance, and governance. Our representation extends to individuals, groups, and institutions seeking redressal for violations of rights or unlawful administrative decisions. We also provide guidance on Public Interest Litigations (PILs) and other constitutional petitions, ensuring that filings comply with procedural rules and relevant case law.

Environment Law

We provide representation and advisory services in matters governed by environmental legislation, including the Environment Protection Act, Water and Air Acts, Wildlife Protection Act, and other statutory provisions. We appear before the Supreme Court, High Courts, and National Green Tribunal, handling matters such as environmental clearances, pollution disputes, land-use restrictions, and regulatory compliance. Our practice also involves pre-litigation guidance, drafting petitions or responses, and representation in hearings before authorities and courts. We integrate legal analysis with an understanding of environmental policy and scientific data, ensuring that matters are handled comprehensively and responsibly.

Cybercrime & Technology Law

We provide representation in matters under the Information Technology Act, including identity theft, hacking, online fraud, cyberstalking, data breaches, and other cyber offenses. We appear before all levels of courts and relevant authorities to ensure statutory compliance and effective representation. Our advisory services extend to businesses and individuals for compliance with data protection laws, cybersecurity measures, and digital governance. When necessary, we collaborate with technical experts to ensure cases are accurately supported, while strictly adhering to procedural and statutory requirements.

Banking & Finance Law

We provide advisory and representation in matters relating to debt recovery, cheque dishonor cases under the Negotiable Instruments Act, mortgage disputes, insolvency and bankruptcy proceedings, and compliance with RBI and SEBI regulations. We represent both financial institutions and borrowers in accordance with statutory provisions. Our work also includes drafting agreements, reviewing financial transactions, and advising on regulatory compliance. All matters are handled in courts and tribunals across India, including appellate forums.

Intellectual Property Rights (IPR)

We provide representation in matters related to trademarks, copyrights, patents, industrial designs, and trade secrets. We appear before courts and statutory authorities, handling filing, opposition, infringement, and compliance-related issues. Our advisory services include guidance on IP portfolio management, licensing, and statutory compliance. In disputes involving infringement or passing-off, we ensure that matters are addressed methodically, in accordance with applicable laws and procedures.

Dispute Resolution (ADR)

We also provide support in resolving disputes through Alternative Dispute Resolution mechanisms, including arbitration, mediation, and negotiation. Our work covers domestic and international arbitration proceedings, settlement agreements, and coordination with arbitration tribunals. Our practice in ADR is conducted in accordance with the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996, and other relevant statutory provisions. By facilitating structured negotiation and arbitration, we assist parties in resolving disputes efficiently, while maintaining compliance with procedural and legal requirements.
